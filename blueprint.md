# **App Name**: NutriSaathi AI

## Core Features:

- Personalized Food Analysis: Users can search for a food product or brand to receive a comprehensive health assessment, including a personalized health score (0-100), consumption decision (Buy/Moderate/Avoid), safe quantity, specific avoidance warnings, and an AI-generated concise explanation, considering their user profile and the Indian food database. The AI also provides tailored suggestions for healthier alternatives as a tool.
- User Profile Management: Allows users to input and manage personal details such as age, weight, existing health conditions, and diet type, which are then used to personalize all food health assessments and recommendations.
- Cart Health Assessment: Enables users to enter multiple food items to simulate a shopping cart and receive an overall cart health score. This feature provides an AI-generated risk summary and smart tool suggestions for healthier replacements tailored to the combined items.
- Custom Indian Food Database: A curated, Nextjs-backed database containing essential nutritional information (name, calories, sugar, sodium, fat, protein, processing level) for common Indian food products to power accurate health scoring and assessments.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A fresh, natural green (#3EB03E) symbolizing health and vitality. This color serves as the main interactive element accent.
- Background color: A subtly tinted off-white (#FAFFF9) with a hint of green, maintaining a clean 'white background' while adding warmth and sophistication, and ensuring good contrast with primary elements.
- Accent color: A soft, inviting sky-blue (#85CCE0) for highlights and secondary interactive elements, harmonizing with the primary green to reinforce themes of freshness and modern technology.
- Headline and body text font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif for its modern, clean, and highly readable characteristics, suitable for a tech-based and user-friendly experience across all text elements.
- Utilize clean, modern line-based icons with rounded edges, complementing the app's 'modern startup style' and 'rounded cards' aesthetic, while clearly representing food and health-related concepts.
- Design with a 'mobile friendly' approach, prioritizing a clean, spacious layout on a light background. Emphasize content hierarchy through 'rounded cards' for search results, cart items, and user profile information to enhance readability and visual appeal.
- Incorporate subtle and smooth transitions for state changes and data loading, along with elegant micro-interactions, to create a 'curiosity-based' and 'attractive' user experience without distractions.
